# Altitude Timesheet — Employee Guide

**App URL:** https://altitudeco.netlify.app/

## First-Time Setup

Your administrator will create an account for you and give you a **4-digit PIN**. You will need this PIN every time you sign in.

## Install on Your Phone

- **iPhone:** Open the URL in Safari → tap Share → **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android:** Open the URL in Chrome → tap menu → **Install app**

## Sign In

1. Open the app
2. Pick your name from the list
3. Enter your 4-digit **PIN**
4. Tap **Sign In**

If you forget your PIN, ask your administrator to reset it.

## Fill Out Your Timesheet

1. Use ◀ ▶ to pick the correct **week**
2. For each workday:
   - Pick the **Job** from the dropdown
   - Enter **Start** and **End** times
   - Add **Lunch** minutes if you took a break
3. Hours are calculated automatically

### Shortcuts

- 📋 **Copy** — copies last week's pattern into this week (it looks back up to 4 weeks if last week is empty)
- ⚡ **Quick Fill** — fills Mon–Fri with 8h on a single job (great for standard weeks)

## Submit Your Timesheet

When the week is done, tap **✅ Submit Week** at the top.

- The app checks for problems first (hours with no job, empty week, etc.)
- Once submitted, the week is **locked** — you can't change it
- The banner turns green and shows when you submitted

If you need to change a submitted week, ask your admin to **Reopen** it.

## My History

Tap **📁 My History** to see every week you've ever submitted, with status badges and an XLSX download for each one.

## Other Buttons

- **📧 Email** — opens your email app with the timesheet attached
- **⬇ XLSX** — downloads this week as an Excel file
- **📄 PDF** — opens print dialog (choose Save as PDF)

## Tips

- Your work is **saved automatically** — no save button
- Works **offline** — fill it in anywhere; it'll upload when you're back online
- One timesheet per week — switch weeks with the arrows
- Tap your name in the header to **sign out**
